About the Game

A block puzzle game featuring a rich booster and economy system. Players place and clear blocks across hundreds of levels, unlocking boosters and managing in-game currency to progress through the mansion-themed world.

My Responsibilities

  • Worked with Spine animations and Unity Timeline for cinematic sequences.
